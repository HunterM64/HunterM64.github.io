About HunterM64

I am an aspiring software engineer!

Currently working on my Bachelor's of Science in Computer Science and Engineering at OSU! Expected graduation is May 2023!

My hobbies include photography, listening to music, playing video games, and reading, and programming!

I got into computer science and programming sometime around middle or high school. It probably started when one of my English teachers lent me the book "The Innovaters" by Walter Isaacson. I had done a book report on "Steve Jobs", another book done by this author, and she thought that I would enjoy reading it due to my interest in computers. Needless to say, I very much enjoyed the book and now have two copies of my own- a physical and digital copy.

Later on in high school, I discovered the joys of programming when someone showed me how he could program his calculator to do math equations for him. I quickly started to learn how to do the same, and had a fun time experimenting with anything else I could program my TI-84 to do. I was also introduced to Scratch in one of my computer courses, and quickly became addicted to that as well. It was from there that I started to look more into things and decided that computer science and engineering was something that I wanted to pursue academically and professionally.
